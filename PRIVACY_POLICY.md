# Privacy Policy for UNIPA Assignment Sync

**Effective Date:** 2026/05/07

## 1. Information We Collect
UNIPA Assignment Sync ("the Extension") extracts assignment data (course names, assignment titles, deadlines, and submission status) exclusively from the assignment list page of your university's academic system (UNIPA).

## 2. How We Use Your Information
The extracted data is used strictly for the single purpose of synchronizing your assignments with your Google Calendar and Google Sheets. The Extension does not collect, use, or store any personal information, browsing history, or passwords.

## 3. Data Storage and Transfer
All extracted assignment data is sent directly from your browser to a Google Apps Script Webhook URL that **you** provide. 
- **The developer of this Extension does NOT receive, collect, or have access to any of your data.**
- Your data is never sent to any third-party servers. 
- Your Webhook URL and notification settings are stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`).

## 4. Third-Party Sharing
We do not share, sell, or rent any of your data to third parties. Your data remains entirely within your own Google account infrastructure.
